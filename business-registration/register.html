Summary of required information

Selected registrations:

  • Australian Business Number (ABN)
  • Business Name
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST)
  • Pay as you go withholding (PAYG)
  • Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT)

You will need to have the following information to complete your application:

  • The names you will be using for your the new entities you will be creating
  • Names and addresses of all business associates
  • Your individual Tax File Number (TFN) to confirm your identity
  • etc.

The application will be saved as you progress, so you will be able to come back later if you don't have all the required information from the start.
