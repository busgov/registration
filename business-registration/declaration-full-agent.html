Declaration to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) and the Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)

Privacy

The information provided to business.gov.au in this form for transmittal to ASIC and ATO may include personal information. All three organisations comply with their obligations under the Privacy Act 1988. The privacy policy pages for business.gov.au, ASIC and ATO describe how your personal information is handled, your rights to seek access to and correct personal information, and how to complain about breaches of privacy.

Authority

If you are not the applicant, you must be authorised by the applicant to make each of the declarations on behalf of the applicant and to submit this application. See who may be authorised to lodge an application to register a business name for more information. It is an offence to provide false or misleading information to ASIC.
