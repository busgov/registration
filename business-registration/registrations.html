You don’t need an ABN to work as an apprentice, trade assistant, or labourer. You work under the direction of your employer, so you’re actually an employee.

If your employer is asking you to register as a contractor when you’re really an employee, you can report the business to the Tax Office.

If you’re not sure whether you’re an employee or not, try the Tax Office’s Employee/contractor decision tool.
