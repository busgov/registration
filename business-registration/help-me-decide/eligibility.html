Registration discovery

Are you starting a business, or thinking about starting one?

If so, this tool can help you work out:

  • the business structure that will best suit your needs
  • the basic business and tax registrations you should consider applying for.

Legal things we need to say...

Business decisions should not be entered into lightly. Many factors can affect your decisions including your finances, legal requirements, industry group and your own personal circumstances. While we make every effort to ensure that this tool is accurate and up to date, it can’t possibly take all your circumstances into account – so please treat it as a guide only.

Before you make any decisions based on the information in this tool, consider seeking independent professional advice as we can’t guarantee or accept legal liability as a result of using this tool.

Not sure if you need to register a business?

We can help you make a decision about whether we would consider you to be in business and what you might need to do next.
