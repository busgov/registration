Application summary

Where to now?

There are a number of registrations you may still need before you can start trading. State and territories have various licences and permits that are often required prior to starting your business or company.

See the Australian Business Licence and Information Service (ABLIS) (opens in new window) to find the licences and permits you need to run your business.

How do I change my form details?

If you’ve made a mistake in your company or business name, you must notify ASIC (opens in new window) within 24 hours after the registration. Generally if there’s a typing error (e.g. letters are mixed up or missing), ASIC will correct it.

If your details have changed, you have 28 days from when you were aware of the change to:

  • update your tax registration details (opens in new window)
  • update your business name details (opens in new window)
  • make changes to your company (opens in new window).

Are there penalties for incorrect information?

There are penalties if the information entered is false or misleading. However, if you let the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) or Australian Securities & Investments Commission (ASIC) know as soon as you're aware of the error or if you update them before they contact you, you may not receive a penalty.

See the Making a false or misleading statement (opens in new window) page on the Australian Business Register (ABR) website.

For business name or company details, see the Late fees (opens in new window) section of the ASIC website.

Where can I register for a business TFN?

If you're registering an ABN for a partnership or company, you'll need a business TFN.

The good news is you don't need to register. The ATO will automatically issue a partnership or company with a TFN (if one doesn't already exist).

Where do I renew my business name?

See the different options available for renewing your business name (opens in new window) on the ASIC website.

Search help

Simple search

The simple search only contains a Search for field. You can enter either an application reference (found at the top left of each Application summary box), Australian Business Number (ABN), Australian Company Number (ACN), company name or business name in this field and select the Find applications button to search.

Tip: You don’t need to enter the details in full. The search will accept partial names.

Please enter the data in the following format:

  • Application reference: e.g. 1234-AB-5678-C (use dashes '-' and remove the hash '#')
  • ABN: e.g. 12345678910 (spaces are not accepted)
  • ACN: e.g. 345678910 (spaces are not accepted)
  • Business name: e.g. Jo's Super Plumbing (partial names are accepted)
  • Company name: e.g. Jo's Plumbing Pty Ltd (partial names are accepted).

Advanced search

If you have 3 or more applications, the Advanced search will appear. Simply select the link Show advanced options to display the advanced search options.

In addition to the fields available in the simple search above, the advanced search also allows you to search by submitted date and status of the application, as well as registration types. To narrow down your search, enter one or more of these filters and click the Find applications button.

See the details for each of the advanced search options below:

  • Submitted date from: Enter a date in the format 01/01/2017 (including forward slashes '/') or select the calendar icon to pick a date.
  • Submitted date to: Enter a date in the format 31/12/2017 (including forward slashes '/') or select the calendar icon to pick a date.
  • Status of application: Use the arrow icon in the right-hand side of the box to select the Not submitted, Payment in Progress, Payment Declined, Submitted or Completed status. If you aren't sure, leave the Show all option.
  • Registration types: Select one or more types from Business name, Company, ABN, GST, PAYG, FBT, LCT, FTC, WET, and AUSKey.

How do I return to the simple search?

Select the Hide advanced options link.

How do I return to my full list of applications?

Remove all the information entered in search fields and select Find applications.

How do I browse my applications?

If you're browsing multiple search results, scroll to the bottom of the screen and select the Show more button to reveal more results.

If the Show more button doesn't appear, you've reached the end of your results.
