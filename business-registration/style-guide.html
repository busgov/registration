08 Navigation

Due to the complexity of the application process (particularly company registration), the user interface has been designed to cater for sections within pages. Rather than bombarding the user with a long list of form controls some pages are divided into logical sections. Only one section is presented at one time. Once all sections have valid data the applicant can move onto the next step in the overall process. Example of navigation
