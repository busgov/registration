What if my details change?

If you have made an error or your details have changed, you have 28 days to update your details with the ABN registrar (opens in new window).

Are there penalties for incorrect information?

There are penalties if the information entered is false or misleading. However, if you let ATO or ASIC know as soon as you're aware of the error or if you update them before they contact you, you may not receive a penalty.

See the Making a false or misleading statement (opens in new window) page on the Australian Business Register (ABR) website.

What happens to my form after I submit?

Once you accept the declaration and submit the form. Your application will be sent to the ATO and or ASIC to be processed.

Can I access my form details after I submit?

No, once you submit the form, you'll no longer have access to the details in your form. These will be deleted from the Business registration website.

How can I check on the progress of my application?

The status of your application can be checked on the Dashboard. Here you'll see whether your application was successful. You'll also be able to see the reason if it was not successful.

What is the payment for?

Payment will be required if you have registered one or more business names, or you have registered a company.

What happens to my payment?

Your payment details will be forwarded securely to the Australian Securities & Investments Commission (ASIC) for processing. Once your payment is processed, our system will receive a receipt number and whether the payment was successful from ASIC. These details will then be displayed on the Dashboard.
