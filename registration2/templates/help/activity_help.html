Have business turnover of $75,000 or more in your first year

When we say business turnover, we mean your gross business income (not your profit). Visit the ATO website for more information on when you need to register for GST.

Charge passengers for taxi services

This includes ride sharing services. This doesn’t include car hire services where you don’t provide a driver. Visit the ATO website for more information on when you need to register for GST.

Charge passengers for limousine travel

This doesn’t include limousine hire services where you don’t provide a driver. Visit the ATO website for more information on when you need to register for GST.

Import or sell wines

When we say wines, we mean grape wine, grape wine products such as vermouth, fruit or vegetable wines, cider, perry, mead, or sake, that has more than 1.15% ethyl alcohol. Visit the ATO website for more information on when Wine Equalisation Tax applies to you.

Use taxable fuels in factories, equipment, machinery or heavy vehicles

When we say heavy vehicles, we mean any vehicle that weighs more than 4.5 tonnes.

Taxable fuels include:

  • liquid fuels:
    • petrol
    • diesel
    • kerosene
    • mineral turpentine
    • white spirit
    • toluene
    • heating oil
    • some solvents
  • gaseous fuels:
    • liquefied petroleum gas (LPG)
    • liquefied natural gas (LNG)
    • compressed natural gas (CNG)

Visit the ATO website for more information on whether Fuel Tax Credits apply to you.

Import or sell luxury cars

Luxury cars are cars that are worth more than $63,184 (more than $75,375 for fuel-efficient vehicles). Visit the ATO website for more information on whether Luxury Car Tax applies to you.
