Styleguide form layout

Form layout uses a 12 column responsive grid system. 12 is divisible by 2 and 3 offering flexible layout options for a range of form inputs. Layout will linearise to single column on narrow devices. Validation can be added to legends and labels to ensure feedback is read by screen readers in forms mode.
