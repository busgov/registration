Legal things we need to say…

Business decisions should not be entered into lightly. Many factors can affect your decisions including your finances, legal requirements, industry group and your own personal circumstances. While we make every effort to ensure that this tool is accurate and up to date, it can’t possibly take all your circumstances into account – so please treat it as a guide only.

Before you make any decisions based on the information in this tool, consider seeking independent professional advice. This tool is not a legal professional, so we can’t guarantee or accept legal liability as a result of using this tool.
